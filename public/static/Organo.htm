Co-creating eco-habitats that celebrate living

Organo builds full-featured sustainable living environments harmlessly integrated into the natural world, supportive of a healthy way of living.

To counter the drawbacks of urbanization, Organo has taken up the challenge of developing eco-habitats thriving in balanced harmony integrated with nature, offering a rurban living experience.

Rurban way of living

The benefits of urbanization and the feel of a rural environment brings together the best of both worlds. Organo offers an integrated alternate-living experience where residents could take part in organic farming and other collective activities, while maintaining their private space.

Sustainability on all fronts

Sustainability, biodiversity, harnessing natural resources and organic living are at the driving principles of Organo. Sapthapatha, or the ‘Seven strands of sustainable living’ help us individually focus on sustainable development in each of the distinct areas.

A conscious community

We focus on building communities that grows to be conscious, conscientious and evolutionary; one that not only takes but gives back. We strive to create the right balance between private and personal life for the people in our community.

Triple-bottom-line focus

Our core philosophy of Samvriddhi (prosperity for all) helps us create replicable community development models built on triple bottom line success (social, environmental and economic areas) and net zero thinking.
